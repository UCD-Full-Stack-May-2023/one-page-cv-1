About Him

George Carp is a web designer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. He has entry level of experience in designing and developing websites for various clients, and is also an experienced healthcare practitioner . He is proficient currently in HTML, CSS following JavaScript, and WordPress, and has a keen eye for aesthetics and usability. George enjoys working with clients to understand their needs and goals, and delivering solutions that exceed their expectations. He is always eager to learn new skills and technologies, and to challenge himself with new projects. When he is not coding or designing, George likes to spend his time motorcycling, driving, or playing video games.
